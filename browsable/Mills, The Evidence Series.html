Elizabeth Shown Mills, CG, CGL, FASG is the author of //Evidence Explained: Citing History Sources from Artifacts to Cyberspace//, 2nd edition (2009) (we often refer to either edition at BetterGEDCOM as Evidence Explained or EE). She earlier authored the classic //Evidence! Citation & Analysis for the Family Historian// (1997). She has also authored several short, laminated guides, each a QuickSheet related to evidence, citations and/or problem solving:

QuickSheet: Citing Online Historical Resources Evidence! Style. First Revised Edition (2007)
//QuickSheet: Citing Ancestry.com Databases & Images//. 1st Revised Edition (2010)
//QuickSheet: Genealogical Problem Analysis//; described as "A Strategic Plan Evidence! Style." (2010)
//QuickSheet: Citing Online African-American Historical Resources Evidence! Style// (2010)

Several vendors have incorporated support for Mills Evidence style in Applications.

There are several BetterGECOM wiki pages dedicated to Mills work.
