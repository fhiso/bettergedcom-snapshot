This section is for people to share their experiences in doing genealogical research. Please create a page and share whatever you want to. This page was inspired by Adrian Bruce's account of his experiences in doing family history research in the UK. However, everyone is encouraged to share their stories, concerns or whatever you might wish to tell. I promise you, the information you choose to share will be new to someone, even if you think it's mundane.

Should you want to add your story, experiences or concerns, just click the "New Page" link on the very top of the left sidebar, give your page a name (preferably including your name or username) and start typing. We will make sure you page is linked here.
