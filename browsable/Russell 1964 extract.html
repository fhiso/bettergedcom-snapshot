Mr. Russell's 1964 article in NGSQ:*


..2. Hans Nicolas Feuerstein, born 25 march 1712, at Berg,
Alsace, was--according to family tradition--a son of Hans Feuerstein.
[...] Because of the loss
by fire of the Berg Lutheran Church Records in 1720, it has not been
possible to trace the ancestry of Hans Nicolas Feuerstein. He and Cath-
arina Nunnemacher were married on 10 April 1733 at the Berg Lu-
theran Church, at which the births of most of their children were re-
corded.^8 [p. 241]
