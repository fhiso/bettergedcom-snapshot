BetterGEDCOM Terminology Proposals


This page contains proposlas for new terms specific to BetterGEDCOM, or changes to existing ones.
Use the Discussion tab above to discuss the proposals.

Add proposals in alphabetical order. Please create a discussion topic with the term and the proposal date as Subject.
For example "Attribute --- 27 Feb 2011".

Resolved proposals are moved to the bottom of the page, the Archive(in alphabetical order). For each term there may be older proposals for the same term in the archive. When a proposal is resolved, the result is added to the BG Specific Terminology page.
