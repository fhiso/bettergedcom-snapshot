Attribute (last updated 12 Dec 1234 by xxx)
An Attribute is any property, fact, characteristic, condition, or trait of an Better GEDCOM entity that has enough genealogical or historical or personal significance that a genealogist or family historian would wish to record that information about the entity.

BG = BetterGEDCOM

Characteristic
A Characteristic is an attribute (q.v.)

Event
An Event is an occurrence in time and space that affects the lives of one or more persons. An event may serve to materially change the life status of the persons, or may to serve to establish an important relationship between the persons or between the persons and some other entities.
